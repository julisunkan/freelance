── LONG DESCRIPTION (landing page / app store) ──────────────────────────────

  Winning freelance work starts with a great proposal — but writing one from
  scratch is time-consuming and often inconsistent. Proposal Builder solves that.

  Powered by Groq AI, Proposal Builder writes high-quality, persuasive freelance
  proposals for you based on a few key details: the client's name, the project
  description, your budget, and timeline. The result is a fully structured
  proposal with a warm introduction, a clear articulation of the client's problem,
  your proposed solution, your deliverables, your investment ask, and a confident
  closing — all in under 30 seconds.

  Already have a draft? Use the "Improve with AI" feature to have Groq refine
  your existing content for tone, persuasiveness, and professionalism without
  changing your core message.

  KEY FEATURES:
  - AI proposal generation powered by Groq (llama-3.3-70b-versatile)
  - Rich WYSIWYG editor (Quill) for full manual control
  - 10 industry proposal templates (Web Dev, Mobile App, Brand Design, SEO,
    Social Media, E-Commerce, UI/UX, Data Analysis, Content Writing, Consulting)
  - Export to PDF, Word (.docx), or plain text
  - Unique shareable link for each proposal — no client login required
  - Proposal status tracking: Draft → Sent → Accepted / Rejected
  - Proposal Strength Score: rates clarity, structure, pricing, and CTA out of 100
  - Full export history log per proposal
  - Admin panel for AI configuration
  - Installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA)
