── LONG DESCRIPTION (landing page / app store) ──────────────────────────────

  Whether you're a founder sharing your pitch deck, a developer starting a new
  client project, or an executive negotiating a merger — you need an NDA before
  the conversation begins. NDA Generator AI makes it effortless.

  Choose from 20 industry-specific templates crafted for the most common
  professional contexts: startups, corporate disclosures, freelance development,
  healthcare (HIPAA-aware), financial services, employment, M&A, venture capital,
  legal services, and more. Each template includes purpose-built confidential
  information definitions, optional compliance clauses, and the right legal tone
  for its industry.

  Optionally activate Groq AI enhancement to refine the legal language in one of
  four tones: Formal, Strict, Startup-Friendly, or Legal-Heavy. The AI
  strengthens structure and clarity while preserving all class names and
  formatting — giving you a more polished agreement without losing control.

  Once generated, both parties receive unique signing links. Each party signs
  using a digital signature pad directly in the browser — no printing, no
  scanning. When both parties have signed, the NDA is locked, and a full PDF
  with embedded signatures, IP addresses, and timestamps is ready to download.

  KEY FEATURES:
  - 20 industry-specific NDA templates (Startup, Corporate, Freelance, Healthcare,
    Finance, Employment, Supply Chain, Research, Real Estate, Marketing, Creative,
    Manufacturing, Legal, Technology Transfer, Investment, M&A, Product, 
    International, Consulting, Software Development)
  - Optional clauses: Non-Compete, IP Assignment, Non-Solicitation, Arbitration,
    Return of Information
  - AI enhancement via Groq — four legal tones (Formal, Strict, Startup, Legal-Heavy)
  - Digital e-signature pad for both parties — browser-based, no software needed
  - Mutual or one-way NDA configuration
  - 195 jurisdictions (all countries worldwide)
  - Duration options: 1, 2, 3, 5 years, or indefinitely
  - PDF export with embedded signatures and metadata
  - Full audit trail: creation, viewing, signing events with timestamps and IPs
  - Unique document ID per NDA for reference
  - Admin panel for AI model and tone configuration
  - Installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA)
